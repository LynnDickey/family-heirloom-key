Welcome to Family Heirloom Key! This guide will walk you through creating and managing your encrypted family heirlooms with inheritance capabilities.

## üè† Creating Your First Heirloom

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

1. Visit the [Family Heirloom Key dApp](https://family-heirloom-key.vercel.app/)
2. Click "Connect Wallet" in the top-right corner
3. Select your preferred wallet
4. Approve the connection request

### Step 2: Create a Family Heirloom

1. Click the "Create Family Heirloom" button
2. Fill in the heirloom details:
   - **Title**: A meaningful name for your heirloom (1-100 characters)
   - **Description**: Detailed description of the heirloom (1-500 characters)
   - **Heir Address**: Ethereum address of your designated heir
   - **Inheritance Deadline**: When inheritance becomes available (1-100 years from now)
   - **Value**: The monetary value in ETH (encrypted and private)

3. Review all information carefully
4. Click "Create Heirloom" and confirm the transaction

### Step 3: Manage Your Heirlooms

- View all your heirlooms in the main dashboard
- Each heirloom card shows creation date, heir, and inheritance status
- Track inheritance deadlines and request status

## üîê Understanding Privacy & Security

### Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE)

- **Private Values**: Heirloom monetary values are encrypted and never revealed
- **Secure Verification**: Inheritance requests use encrypted proof-of-life
- **Zero-Knowledge**: The system verifies conditions without exposing sensitive data

### Security Best Practices

- **Backup Your Seed Phrase**: Never share your wallet's recovery phrase
- **Verify Addresses**: Always double-check heir addresses before creation
- **Use Strong Passwords**: Protect your wallet with strong security
- **Keep Software Updated**: Use the latest versions of wallets and browsers

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Transaction Failed"
- **Cause**: Insufficient gas or network congestion
- **Solution**: Try increasing gas limit or waiting for network to clear

#### "Invalid Heir Address"
- **Cause**: The provided address is not a valid Ethereum address
- **Solution**: Verify the address format (should start with 0x and be 42 characters)

#### "Inheritance Deadline Too Soon"
- **Cause**: Deadline must be at least 1 year from creation
- **Solution**: Choose a future date at least 365 days from now

#### "Contract Paused"
- **Cause**: Emergency pause activated by contract owner
- **Solution**: Wait for the contract to be unpaused or contact support

#### "Wallet Connection Failed"
- **Cause**: Network issues or wallet configuration problems
- **Solution**: Refresh the page, check your wallet connection, or try a different browser

## üìä Understanding Gas Fees

### What are Gas Fees?

Gas fees are transaction costs on the Ethereum network, paid in ETH to miners/validators.

### Fee Estimation

- **Creating Heirloom**: ~0.01-0.05 ETH
- **Setting New Heir**: ~0.005-0.02 ETH
- **Inheritance Request**: ~0.008-0.03 ETH
- **Approving Inheritance**: ~0.007-0.025 ETH

### Optimizing Gas Usage

- **Network Timing**: Transact during off-peak hours for lower fees
- **Gas Limit**: The dApp automatically estimates appropriate gas limits
- **Layer 2**: Consider using Polygon or Arbitrum for lower fees
